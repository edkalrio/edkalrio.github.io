Frases Célebres VI

  • 2010-04-06
"I've seen Natal, and I'm really curious to see what they're going to do with it. I was a bit frustrated that they presented it with a sci-fi-type presentation. I thought it was a little bit lying to present it that way, because it will let consumers think that they will see something that they are not really close to getting."
(He visto Natal, siento verdadera curiosidad por ver qué es lo que han hecho con esto. Me cabreó un poco que su presentación fuera de ciencia ficción. Pensé que era como si estuvieran mintiendo, en cierto modo, porque eso provocaría que los consumidores pensaran que iban a ver algo que no se iba a lograr.)
David Cage, copresidente de Quantic Dream (creadores de Heavy rain).
